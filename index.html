The importance of Work-Life balance

Rob Vogan

Thursday, December 2nd 2015

What is Work-Life balance?

  • According to the Canadian Mental health association, it is a prioritizing of the proper things at the proper time

  • In general terms, work-life balance is managing yoru time to fit both work and social/family activities

  • For example alotting enough time to finish a project before leaving for a friend's birthday

Work Life balance is relevant to Business Technology in numerous ways, but mainly in managing work volume


- While managing both business functions and technology issues such as IT problems or crashes, it is easy to lose track of time and other prior commitments


- Can you think of a time when you found yourself slacking or forgetting about a school assignment due to other activities?

In conclusion, balancing a working lifestyle with a social lifestyle is almost critical to overall success, and everyone has their own way of doing so
